Requirements to Begin?


To begin there aren't many requirements. As a beginner you really only need a...

  1. Camera
  2. Computer
  3. Photo Editing Software
  4. Microphone (only if doing cinematography)
  5. Passion for photography/cinematography

The last on list is the most important. It doesn't matter what kind of equipment you have if you have no passion. You can even begin working with your phone, as long as you have passion for it. Atleast, that's how I began, just me and my Samsung Galaxy 6 Edge. (The first 4 images in the "Photographs" tab are actually photos taken on a Samsung Galaxy 6 Edge.)
